🔒 Privacy Policy (GDPR Compliance) ▼

Last Updated: May 2026. This Privacy Policy explains how ChessLense Analytics GmbH collects, uses, and discloses your information.

1. Data Collection and Usage

We collect personal data only when strictly necessary for the operation of our services. When you create an account, we collect your full name and email address to manage your subscription and authenticate your identity (Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR - Performance of a Contract).

2. Handling of Payment and Bank Data

ChessLense Analytics GmbH explicitly does not process, transmit, or store your bank details, credit card numbers, or sensitive financial information on our servers. We utilize a PCI-DSS Level 1 compliant third-party payment processor (Stripe) to handle all financial transactions. When you enter your payment information during the checkout process, the data is encrypted and sent directly from your browser to Stripe. We only receive a secure token confirming the success of the transaction and the last four digits of the card for billing reference purposes.

3. User-Generated Content (PGN Files)

Any Portable Game Notation (PGN) files you upload for analysis are securely transmitted using TLS encryption. These files are processed on our servers located in Frankfurt, Germany. We do not sell your game data to third parties. Anonymized move data may be aggregated to improve our heuristic engine.

4. Cookies and Analytics

We respect your privacy by avoiding intrusive tracking. We strictly use only essential session cookies required to maintain your login state. We do not use third-party marketing, retargeting, or advertising cookies. For internal performance monitoring, we employ a self-hosted, privacy-first analytics tool that hashes IP addresses immediately, ensuring no individual user can be tracked across the web.

5. Contact Form Submissions

Information submitted through our support contact form (name, email, and message content) is temporarily stored in our ticketing system solely for the purpose of resolving your inquiry. This data is routinely purged 90 days after the ticket is closed.

6. Your Rights Under GDPR

Under the General Data Protection Regulation, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • The right to access (Art. 15 GDPR)
  • The right to rectification (Art. 16 GDPR)
  • The right to erasure / "Right to be forgotten" (Art. 17 GDPR)
  • The right to restrict processing (Art. 18 GDPR)
  • The right to data portability (Art. 20 GDPR)

To exercise any of these rights, please contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@chesslense-mock.at. We will respond to your request within 30 days.
